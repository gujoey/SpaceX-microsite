SPACEX

Space Exploration Technology Company (SpaceX) is an American aerospace manufacturer and space transportation provider.

The company was founded in 2002 by Elon Musk. His goal is to provide affordable space transportation costs and enabeling the colonization of Mars.

ELON MUSKS MASTERPLAN

Build innovative reusable rockets ✔
Ship other companies stuff to space ✔
Use the money you earn to build bigger rockets ✔
Colonize Mars

FALCON HEAVY ROCKET

SpaceX has reasently successfully launced their Falcon Heavy rocket. Its payload was nothing less than Elon Musks own Tesla Roadster.

JUNE 6
2018

Space craft: Soyuz
Payload: ISS crew

On June 6 the russian goverment will be launching the Souyuz space craft. The payload is an Explorer crew that is going to stay in the ISS for 6 months.

This space craft had an original launch date in april 2018, but the launch was delayed
