SPACEX

Space Exploration Technology Company (SpaceX) is an American aerospace manufacturer and space transportation provider.

The company was founded in 2002 by Elon Musk. His goal is to provide affordable space transportation costs and enabeling the colonization of Mars.

ELON MUSKS MASTERPLAN

1. Build innovative reusable rockets
2. Ship other companies stuff to space
3. Use the money you earn to build bigger rockets
4. Colonize Mars

FALCON HEAVY ROCKET

SpaceX has reasently successfully launced their Falcon Heavy rocket. Its payload was nothing less than Elon Musks own Tesla Roadster.

BIG FALCON ROCKET

Big Falcon Rocket is Elon Musks next masterpiece. It was announced i september 2017, and the development of the prototype started in march 2018. Testing will begin in early 2019.

INTERNATIONAL SPACE STATION

The ISS is an inhabited satelite that is orbiting earth in low earth orbit (2.000 km).
ISS uses aproximately 92.49 minutes to orbit the earth since it's traveling at a speed of aproximately 28.000 km/h. This results in the ISS orbiting the earth 15.5 times times per day!
